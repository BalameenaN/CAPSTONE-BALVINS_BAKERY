E-commerce Platform
-------------------
Description: Build a full-fledged e-commerce platform where users can browse products, add them to a shopping cart, and proceed with secure checkout. Admins should be able to manage products, view orders, and handle inventory.
Tech Stack: MongoDB (product, user, and order data), Express (API routes for managing products and orders), React (front-end shopping experience), Node.js (server-side functionality).
Extra Features: Payment integration (like Stripe), product search and filtering, and customer reviews.


Vegan & Specialty Cakes E-commerce
----------------------------------
Description: Create an e-commerce platform that specializes in vegan, gluten-free, and allergen-friendly cakes, catering to customers with specific dietary preferences.
Features:
Customizable filter options for dietary preferences (vegan, gluten-free, nut-free, etc.)
Product catalog with detailed ingredients and nutritional information
Customer reviews and ratings for each type of specialty cake
User profiles for saving dietary preferences and past orders
Extra: Subscription service for dietary cakes or “cake-of-the-month” based on user preferences.
